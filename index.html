— FAQs

Questions? Look here.

How long should a YouTube script be? +
It depends on your video length. A good rule is 130–150 words per minute. For a 10-minute video, aim for 1300–1500 words. I always write with pacing in mind so the script feels natural when spoken.
Do you provide YouTube scripts with hooks and CTAs? +
Yes! Every script I write includes a strong hook in the first 3–5 seconds, engaging storytelling throughout, and clear calls-to-action (subscribe, like, comment, etc.) at the right moments.
Can I get a script optimized for YouTube SEO? +
Absolutely. I incorporate target keywords naturally into the script, title suggestions, description hooks, and chapter timestamps to help your video rank better in YouTube search.
How fast can you deliver a YouTube script? +
Standard delivery is 2–3 business days for a 8–12 minute script. Rush orders (24 hours) are available for an additional fee depending on complexity.
Do you write scripts for different niches? +
Yes. I write scripts for education, tech reviews, finance, self-improvement, gaming, vlogs, faceless channels, and more. Just share your niche and target audience.
Can I request revisions after delivery? +
Of course! I offer 2 rounds of revisions included in the price. Additional changes can be made if needed to ensure you're 100% satisfied with the final script.
