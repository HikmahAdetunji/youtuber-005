About Hook Engineering

H

Most videos die in the first 3 seconds. Not because the content is bad — but because the hook doesn't do its job. Hook Engineering is a dedicated service where I craft multiple high-converting hook variations for your videos, tested against proven psychological triggers.

Hook Types

The Hooks I Write:

Every video gets multiple hook variations — curiosity gaps, bold claims, open loops, and pattern interrupts — so you can test what converts best for your audience.

Why It Works

Services Benefits:

A great hook doesn't just get views — it trains the algorithm that your content is worth recommending. These hooks are built to improve AVD, CTR, and watch-through rate simultaneously.

Every hook is built on a specific psychological trigger — curiosity, fear of missing out, social proof, or pattern interruption — that's proven to stop thumb-scrolling cold.
Getting multiple hook variations per video means you can A/B test your thumbnail and first frame copy to find the highest-converting combination.
Hooks are aligned with your thumbnail concept so your visual and verbal first impression work together — dramatically improving click-through rate from search and suggested.
